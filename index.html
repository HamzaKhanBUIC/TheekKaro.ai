Why TheekKaro?

"Bureaucracy is slow; citizens are fast. I built TheekKaro.ai for the AI Seekho 2026 challenge to bridge the gap between local hazards and government action. Powered by Next-Gen AI.

👁️

Multimodal Hazard Detection

Utilizing Google's latest Gemini 2.0 Flash architecture, TheekKaro analyzes complex video feeds and high-res images to instantly classify civic hazards and assess severity.

📍

Precision Civic Routing

Integrated GPS reverse-geocoding automatically pinpoints the hazard's exact street address and identifies the specific local authority responsible for the fix.

📝

Bilingual Auto-Filing

Bypasses language barriers by instantly generating legally formatted, ready-to-send official complaints in both English and native Urdu script.
